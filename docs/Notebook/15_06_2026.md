## 15/06/2026

What does the basic template synthesiser look like?  Not a finished synth.

KISS. Control-plane SOC and test audio pipeline for board bring-up.  A simple single oscillator. 8-voice polyphony.  Enough to prove the board, voice management, modulators and MIDI works.

**SOC Control Plane Functions**

- Voice Management
- MIDI parsing 
- Parameter updates
- Control Rate Signals (modulators)
  - LFO (2)
  - ADSR EG (2)
  - Performance controls (modwheel, bend, portamento, sus pedal)
  

**Audio Pipeline Functions**

- Sample generation (DSP)
- Audio output (I2S)
